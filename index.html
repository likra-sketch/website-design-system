Styles

H1: Main Heading

Font-family: Fjalla One || Font-size: 48px || Font-color: yellow-red-gradient || Font-weight: 700 || Text-transform: uppercase

Note: the gradient color works best for shorter, centred headings. If headings are long and cannot be centred, default to color "dirty orange" to ensure sufficient foreground/background contrast.

Font-family: Fjalla One || Font-size: 24px || Font-color: dark-gray || Font-weight: 700 || Text-transform: uppercase
